02 / intern life

What Interns
Actually Do.

Not just fetching coffee. Mostly raising tickets and waiting for access.

01

Raise a Ticket

Need repo access? Ticket. Need to read a doc? Ticket. Need to exist in the building? There is probably a form. ETA: 3 to 6 business weeks, no promises.

02

Wait for Access

By the time your access gets approved you have already Googled the answer, asked Claude, written the feature, and deployed it. The ticket is still "In Review."

03

Daily Standups

"What did you do yesterday?" Waited for access. "What are you doing today?" Waiting for access. "Any blockers?" Yes. The access. Same answer every day.

04

Wednesday Lunch

The one day the office is actually full. Free food, good vibes, and a 45-minute conversation about whether the new IAM policy breaks prod. It does.
